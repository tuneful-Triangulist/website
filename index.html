Research interests

I'm interested in quantum complexity theory and cryptography; I mostly work on quantum proof (and argument) systems. Topics that interest me in complexity theory include MIP*, QMA(2) and quantum PCP. In cryptography, I've largely worked on quantum verification, and particularly on the application of MIP* techniques in single-prover settings. I'm currently advised by Anand Natarajan; I did my undergraduate degree at Caltech, and there I worked with Thomas Vidick and Chris Umans.
