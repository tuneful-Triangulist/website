Research interests

My previous projects have largely been in quantum cryptography. I've had the good fortune to do several projects under the guidance of Thomas Vidick, and we've mostly worked together on zero-knowledge proof systems and proofs of knowledge. I was also lucky enough to spend a summer under the mentorship of Chris Umans, with whom I thought on a few (classical) ideas related to the derandomisation of small space computing.
